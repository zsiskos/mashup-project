About Us

This group is for anyone who plays the ukulele. Whether you are a beginner, a seasoned performer, or are just a bit curious, this group is for you.

We meet on the 2nd Wednesday of every month, at The Bishop and the Belcher, at 7:00pm. Don't worry, even though the location is always the same the songs and themes are different each event, so you'll get a chance to play a lot of different songs. They are always prepared in advance and if you want to lead a group song, feel free to submit a song!

Why join?

Practice and get better.

Meet fellow enthusiasts.

It's fun!
